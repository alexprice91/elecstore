Nest Learning Thermostat 3rd Gen

The Nest Thermostat automatically adapts as your life changes. Just use it for a week and it programs itself. The 3rd-generation Nest Learning Thermostat now comes with more advanced features including -

Auto-Schedule

Just turn it up and down. It learns the temperatures that you like and creates a schedule for you.

Home/Away Assist

Don’t waste energy heating an empty home. Nest automatically helps save energy when you're out.

Hot Water

Now you can control your hot water tank. And give your hot water a boost when you need it.

Remote control

Change the temperature and control your hot water tank from anywhere using your mobile, tablet or laptop.

Will Nest work in my home?

Yes

  • Combi Boilers
  • System & Regular Boilers
  • Wet Underfloor Heating
  • Air Source Heat Pumps (Heating Only)
  • Heating systems with OpenTherm technology
  • Domestic hot water tanks

No

  • Electric radiators
  • Electric underfloor heating
  • Electric hot water tanks
